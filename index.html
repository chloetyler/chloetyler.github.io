About

My interest in Web Development began early.

In high school, I was often found spending late nights building blogs about my favourite movies, tv shows and video games. I realized I had truly found my passion while setting up and maintaining the website, Point of Sale system and SEO tools for the small business I was working for.

Enrolling in the two-year Web Development program at NSCC was one of the greatest choices I've ever made. I am grateful to be entering an industry that promotes endless learning, and am excited to continue to gain new skills post-graduation.

My toolboX

So far, I've enjoyed building applications with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP and SQL. I've continued to experiment with useful frameworks, from Laravel to Bootstrap, and appreciate the functionality and efficiency they bring to a project. I have discovered my love for Linux and feel confident in the command line.

As I come to the end of my program, I am most looking forward to my Self Directed Study, where I have selected Angular JS as my topic, and continuing work on my Capstone project, where my team and I are building a custom Wordpress site for a real-world client.
